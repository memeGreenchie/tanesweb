About Us

We are a venture capital firm backing exceptional founders at the earliest stage of innovation.

Tanes is a Singapore-based venture capital firm at the forefront of investing in transformative technologies, including Artificial Intelligence, Web3 infrastructure, and frontier innovations that shape the future. We are deeply committed to supporting the next generation of founders who are not just building companies, but pioneering entirely new categories. With a presence rooted in Asia and a global perspective, we seek out teams with unconventional insight, relentless ambition, and the technical edge to redefine what’s possible.

Our investment thesis centers around emerging technologies that reshape industries. We believe in long-term partnerships, and we work closely with teams to scale vision into reality. From pre-seed to growth, we stay committed as hands-on allies throughout the journey.

Backed by a diverse group of LPs and advisors, Tanes empowers startups with speed, insight, and conviction.
